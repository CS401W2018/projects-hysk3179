Structual Tags

Q1. What goes in the header?

a1. In heading, there are Logo, navigation, etc inside.

Q2. What goes in the Nav? Can the Nav go in the header? Does it have to go in the header? Does the Nav contain links to another website?

a2. It has links to important sections of the site. Therefore, there is something like, 'about us', 'contact page'. / Yes, the Nav can in the header of a webpage. / No, the Nav doesn't have to go in the header. / The Nav can include links to different websites.

Q3. What goes in Main?

a3. It is a major content, so it can be everything that intended for user to see.

Q4. What goes in the Footer?

a4. It is a bottom line of webpage, usually copyright, contact information, etc.
