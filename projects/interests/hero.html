Hero - Lee, Jung-jae

This actor, Lee Jung-jae, is the main character in Squid Game. He is in a disadvantaged position, but he is trying to help everyone for a fair and right society by struggling in Squid Game, risking his life. He is hated by people, but he doesn't give up and move for everyone.
