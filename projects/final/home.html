About Family Tree About Cho's Family

A family tree is a diagram that shows the relationships between different members of a family, usually tracing generations. It visually represents how people are related through bloodlines, marriages, and other familial connections.

Taking REL 261 last semester, I was able to expand my family tree and fill the 4th generation family members. I wanted to make a site through this where I could show my family tree.

In Korea, even after getting married, women do not take their husband's last name.
