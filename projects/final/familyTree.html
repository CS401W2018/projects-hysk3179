About Family Tree

A family tree is a visual representation of an extended family, based on names, dates, places, and relationships. It shows how individuals are connected across different generations.

Family records in the world

USA : The National Archives and Records Administration (NARA) is the nation's record keeper, preserving permanently valuable records of the U.S. federal government.

National Archives Website

Korea : Jokbo is a Korean family genealogy book that records the lineage, ancestry, and important information about a family's male descendants, often going back many generations. It includes names, birth and death dates, places of origin, and sometimes achievements or roles in society. Traditionally, only male family members were recorded, reflecting the patriarchal system of old Korea.
